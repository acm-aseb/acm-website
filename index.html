Who We Are

The ACM Student Chapter at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a vibrant, student-driven technical community dedicated to fostering innovation, critical thinking, and excellence in computing. Operating under the world-renowned Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), our chapter serves as a platform for students to explore technology beyond textbooks.

We actively organize hackathons, technical workshops, coding competitions, expert lectures, and collaborative projects that provide hands-on experience with real-world tools and technologies. Alongside technical growth, we emphasize leadership, teamwork, and professional development, enabling our members to grow as confident innovators and future leaders in the tech industry.
