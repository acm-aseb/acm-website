Algomania 3.0 — Overnight Hackathon

Collaboration Event

ByteRoots

ByteRoots was a collaborative event conducted in association with the Prakriti Club. It was a unique Tech × Treasure Hunt experience where participants solved technical clues and challenges to progress through different stages, combining logical thinking, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.

Outreach Event

Aarohan

Aarohan was an outreach initiative where school students were invited to visit our campus and experience college life. The event featured four interactive technical stalls involving games, puzzles, and logical challenges designed to introduce young minds to technology in an engaging and enjoyable manner.
